# Company Information: Ultrafast AI Corp

## Overview
Ultrafast AI Corp is a leading pioneer in the field of high-performance artificial intelligence infrastructure. Founded in 2024, our mission is to make real-time AI inference accessible to everyone.

## Key Products
- **NeuralChip X1**: A specialized hardware accelerator designed for Large Language Models.
- **SpeedEngine SDK**: A software suite that optimizes transformer models for low-latency environments.
- **FastRAG Cloud**: A managed service for lightning-fast retrieval-augmented generation.

## Locations
Our headquarters is located in Silicon Valley, California, with research labs in Tokyo and London.
